Basic Burger

Description

Simple burger for lazy people that're looking for some good varied food

Original recipe website

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
  2. Whisk egg, salt and pepper together in medium bowl
  3. Add ground beef and bread crumbs, and mix them with either a fork or your hand to blend them
  4. form them by hand into four equal 3/4 inch thick patties
  5. Place the patties the preheated grill. Cover and cook for 6-8 minutes per side,
    or to desired doneness. An instant-read thermometer inserted into their center should
    read at least 70 degrees C
  6. Eat it
